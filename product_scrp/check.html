FAQ’s

Can I track my order?

Yes, by logging on to your account, or alternatively enter the tracking number your received by e-mail here;

 
Which countries do you deliver to?

We ship to over 30 countries. Any issues on the site setting delivery to your country then please get in touch at info@thesportsedit.com. 

How do customs and import charges work?

Any relevant taxes and duties will be calculated according to your shipping destination. UK VAT is charged on all orders dispatched to the United Kingdom. For EU destinations local VAT will be applied.

For US and other outside of EU countries, all relevant import duties will not be included in the prices shown.

Which courier companies do you use?

For UK standard we use Royal Mail, for International delivery we use Royal Mail and DHL in case of larger parcels.
